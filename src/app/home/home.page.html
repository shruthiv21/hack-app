Telemedicine Telemedicine

Telemedicine refers to the practice of caring patients remotely when the provider and patients are not physically present with each other. It allows the patients in remote locations to access medical expertise quickly without travel.

Some features of Telemedicine
  • Appointment scheduling
  • Video conferencing
  • Remote treatment plans
  • Chats/messaging
  • Billing
  • Online forms
